ENERGY AURA
Effect: Damage, (Selective), Faster Action 4 (Triggered) • 6 points per rank
You can surround your body with an aura of damaging energy or some similar effect. Typically anyone you touch or that touches you is attacked by it (it can't miss and is close range) although you may set the conditions. By default this excludes the floor from taking damage but any other objects are not exempt. In any case you may actiavte it at any time as a reaction but keep in mind once you attack you can't attack again until your next turn (see Chapter 8 Action & Adventure: Action Limitations). The aura is always on and can't be turned off by choice for that see any of the modifiers below.

EXTRAS

Resetable Triggers: This modifier allows you to reset the triggering conditions at any time as a free action including the default behavior (ie whether it is on all the time or not). See Faster Action for more details. Flat 2 points

FREE MODIFIERS

Enhanced Trait: Instead of simply buying energy aura you can buy Enhanced Trait: Energy Aura for the same price since Enhanced Trait uses a Free action and is Sustained by default this means you can turn it off and on as a free action. However being Sustained instead of Continuous means that it will deactivate whenever you can't maintain it. 0

FLAWS

Activation: This flaw allows you to turn off the aura as a free action however it requires either a move or standard action to turn on. Move: Flat -1 point. Standard: Flat -2 points
