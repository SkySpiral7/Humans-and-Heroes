A GOD I AM (GENERAL)
Effect: Variable Action: Free • Range: Personal • Duration: Continuous • Cost: 150 points then 5 points per rank
As Variable except that there is no longer a required limitation and that the first rank provides you with a set of 105 character points you can allocate to different effects. Each rank beyond the first cost 5 points and provides another 10 variable character points. You can't use these points to gain any Transcendent advantage or power. The effects gained from these variable character points count as 1 Transcendence lower. The first rank of this power has 9 phantom ranks (see Phantom Ranks).
