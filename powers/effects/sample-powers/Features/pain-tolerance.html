PAIN TOLERANCE
Effect: Feature Action: None • Range: Personal • Duration: Permanent • Cost: 3 points per rank
You feel no pain. Regeneration and Immortality powers still feel pain by default and because of this you may pass out from shear pain or shock even if your HP remains positive. Pain isn't felt if damage isn't dealt and thus characters with high toughness don't have this problem. This feature removes that problem. You still have tactile sense. Technically you still feel pain but you can ignore it to the highest degree that you are completely immune to even the highest forms of physical torture but only against natural pain not illusions or afflictions. Meaning an Affliction power with pain descriptor will surpass this feature. If you'd like a less extreme version of this instead put ranks into Resistance (to pain). This also covers other senses such as very bitter or bad smell this does not make you resistance to anything Sense-Dependent. You can't be immune to mental torture, instead put ranks into Will defense.
Example: Surgery needs to be performed on Mr. Gun to remove his appendix. To make a point he decides to go without pain killers of any fashion since he is immune to pain anyway. He is conscious and completely tactilely aware the entire time but doesn't even flinch. However Mr. Gun's heart beating at regular speed causes bleeding during the process making the surgery much more difficult and caused substantial blood loss.
