HEAVY

Effect: Growth without Strength, Stamina, or size bonuses • 3 points per rank

This is Growth for mass only. This is useful for resisting Trip attempts for example: you get a +1 to resist Trip attempts per mass rank increase.

EXTRAS

Unmovable: Instead of gaining mass you only gain the inertia of increased mass. So that you are as hard to move or trip as if you were heavy but without damaging any floor you stand on (rather your weight is unchanged by using the power). +1 cost per rank.
