RESISTANCE (DEFENSE)

Action: None • Range: Personal • Duration: Permanent • Cost: 3 points per rank
You can better resist certain descriptors. When taken choose a descriptor or a base power to resist. The descriptor chosen can include natural causes such as suffocation. You can't apply the Variable Descriptor modifier to this power. If you have more than one Resistance of the same type then only the highest applies.
When you are affected by that power or descriptor after you roll an activate defense but before you roll a passive defense, roll an opposed power check as though this power was countering that effect, if you succeed you are immune to the effect (but it isn't countered). You are immune in case of a tie. For example, if you have Fire Resistance 5 and you roll a 2 against a rank 9 Fire Damage who rolls -2 then 5+2 vs 9-2 is 7 vs 7 which means you are immune. If you are have more than one Resistance that might make you immune, roll each of them separately. If at least one succeeds you are immune. If you fail all of them roll the passive defense normally.
This power has a maximum rank equal to the power level. Note that this is more worth while than Enhanced Trait: Toughness limited to fire (1 point per 2 ranks) because: you can have both this and high toughness, this will resist effects that are not resisted by toughness, and you get to roll twice (bell curves are safer). This power is cheaper than Triggered action Nullify at the cost of only making you immune instead of ending the effect.

EXTRAS

Area: If you make Resistance an area effect then everyone (or everything) in the area will be immune. The power check for Resistance is only rolled once and the result applies to each subject. See Area for cost and details.
