A specific and useful application of contested rolls is a Damage power check. The chance of success is defined by doing at least 0 damage, that is to say the chance of inflicting a bruised penalty. The statistics can be applied for any rank of Damage or Toughness. For example Damage 10 vs Toughness 5 is a toughness difference of -5 which has a 97.35% that the attacker will succeed.

Note that these numbers can be applied to any contested roll involving traits however the chances assume that there is no automatic success or failure and that you will succeed in the case of a tie. If you lose from a tie then treat your opponent's trait as 1 higher. For example if you are rolling an Affliction 5 power check against a Fortitude of 10 then your chance of success is 2.65%.
