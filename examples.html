EXAMPLES

This page contains additional examples of characters, powers, and devices. Some of the examples are based on material I don't own and it will be labeled where it is from.

Note that there are plenty of characters who have already been converted into M&M 3e the most popular/biggest one is The Atomic Think Tank Forum: Roll Call but there's also Mutants & Masterminds Conversions wikidot. Note that people tend to fall victim to the snafu of meaningless numbers and make the character PL 10. Even if you picked a strength for Superman (DC comics) to be consistent with, that number shouldn't be anywhere near PL 10.

Magic Conch Shell: Sample device from SpongeBob SquarePants "Club SpongeBob" s3e2a/e42a. Attain any Knowledge (5), Easily Removable: -1 points, Misinformation: half the time it tells you to do nothing (granted the plot had it always works out in their favor), Quirk: must be a club member to use it -0 flat point (since joining the club has virtually no requirements). Total: 3 points.
