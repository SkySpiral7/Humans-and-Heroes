Power-lifting

Effect: Limited Enhanced Trait: Strength • Action: None • Range: Personal • Duration: Permanent • Cost: 1 point per rank

Your lifting and carrying capacity is out of proportion with the rest of your Strength. Each rank in this power gives you +1 to your Strength rank for determining how much weight you can lift and carry, but does not increase your Strength-based damage, strength checks (including skills) except for lifting, or other effects of your Strength rank.

Since this doesn't increase damage it is not limited by the Attack & Effect limitation but is still limited by Skill Modifier as described in power level limitations.
