Ingrained Powers

Effect: Feature • Action: None • Range: Personal • Duration: Permanent • Cost: 1 point per rank

Your powers are tied to you and not your body. This means that if anyone uses Mind Switch then all of your powers travel with you and they get none of them. If one of the characters have this power during a Mind Switch then they both do so that niether of you end up with both of the power sets.
