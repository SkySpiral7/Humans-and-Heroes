Invisibility

Effect: Visual Concealment • Cost: 4 or 8 points

You can vanish from sight on demand, gaining total visual concealment, although other senses can still detect you. This power costs 4 points if you are only invisible to normal vision, 8 points if you cannot be detected by any visual sense (including infrared and ultraviolet).
